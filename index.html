✓ Research Wireframes Hi-fi UI ✓ Front-end
switch · state & motion

Every color on this site is generated live from the seed swatch you pick in the top bar — primary, containers, surfaces, and text roles, in light and dark. It's the same tokens-first thinking I bring to product work: define the system once, and every screen stays consistent. And because accessible means provable, every generated palette is contrast-checked at runtime and adjusted until it passes WCAG AA (≥ 4.5:1) — try the teal seed and watch the link color deepen to comply.
